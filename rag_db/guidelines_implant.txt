Implant CBCT Evaluation Guidelines:
- Minimum bone height recommended: 8–10 mm.
- Minimum bone width recommended: 5–6 mm.
- Maintain 2 mm safety distance from the mandibular canal.
- Assess cortical bone thickness.
- Check maxillary sinus floor proximity and pneumatization.
- Look for sinus septa before sinus lift.
- Evaluate bone density pattern: D1–D4 classification.
